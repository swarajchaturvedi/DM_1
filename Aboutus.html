About Us

At Denver MicroSystem, we take pride in being a leading supplier in India, dedicated to providing a comprehensive range of high-quality Active and Passive components. With a commitment to excellence and a passion for innovation, we have positioned ourselves as a go-to source for Diodes, Bridge Rectifiers (THT & SMT), Small Signal Transistors, and a diverse array of capacitors, including Dipped Tantalum Capacitors, Chip Tantalum Capacitors, Metallized Polyester Capacitors, and Multilayer Ceramic Capacitors. Our journey began with a vision to empower industries with reliable and cutting-edge electronic components. Established as a prominent player in the market, Denver MicroSystem has consistently delivered products that meet the highest industry standards. We understand the critical role electronic components play in the seamless functioning of various sectors, including consumer electronics, computing, communications, industrial markets, and military applications.

Our Product Range:


Denver MicroSystem takes pride in offering an extensive and meticulously curated selection of Active and Passive components. Our portfolio includes Active Semiconductors, such as Diodes and Small Signal Transistors, designed to enhance the performance of electronic circuits. We also specialize in providing Bridge Rectifiers in both Through-Hole Technology (THT) and Surface Mount Technology (SMT), ensuring compatibility with diverse applications. On the Passive side, our capacitor range is diverse and meets the stringent demands of modern electronic systems. From Dipped Tantalum Capacitors known for their reliability in various environments to Chip Tantalum Capacitors designed for compact applications, our offerings cater to a wide spectrum of requirements. Metallized Polyester Capacitors and Multilayer Ceramic Capacitors from Denver MicroSystem are known for their stability, precision, and durability.

Industries We Serve:
